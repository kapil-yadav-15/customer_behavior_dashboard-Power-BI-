# customer_behavior_dashboard-Power-BI-

Customer Behavior & Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze customer purchasing behavior, revenue distribution, and sales performance across product categories. It tracks key KPIs including 3.9K total customers, $59.76 average purchase amount, and 3.75 average review rating. The dashboard features revenue breakdowns by category and age group, subscription status analysis, and dynamic slicers for gender, shipping type, and product category filtering. Key findings revealed that Young Adults are the highest revenue-generating segment, Clothing dominates sales volume, and 73% of customers are non-subscribers — presenting a clear opportunity for loyalty-based marketing strategies.
